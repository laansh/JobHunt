# interview-prep

- **Phase:** Run (Gate 4 — Interview; re-run per round)
- **Trigger:** "prep me for {Company}", "interview prep", "I have a first round with {Company}"
- **Reads:** `about-me.md`, `voice-and-style.md`, `project-list.md`, role's `JDSEvaluation` + `JDS`
- **Writes:** `Roles/{Company}-{Role}/InterviewPrep/{Round}-{YYYY-MM-DD}/` (Briefing, Stories, Questions, Glossary, Positioning)
- **Mode:** one-shot per round

---

## Purpose

Get the user ready for a specific scheduled round — phone screen, hiring manager, panel, technical,
exec, or final. Re-run for each round; later rounds build on earlier ones.

## Instructions

1. Confirm the round type, date, format, and interviewers if known. Create
   `InterviewPrep/{Round}-{YYYY-MM-DD}/` in the role folder.
2. Read the deep eval (fit analysis, pathway, risks) and `project-list.md`.
3. Produce the materials below. Tailor depth to the round: phone screens stay light; panels/finals go deep.
4. **STAR stories:** select the 4–6 strongest from `project-list.md` that match this posting's themes;
   write each in STAR form with a crisp metric. Map each story to likely questions.
5. **Positioning script:** a tight "why me / why this role / why now" tied to the company's specific
   need from the JDS and deep eval. On-voice per `voice-and-style.md`.
6. **Questions to ask:** sharp, role/company-specific — not generic.
7. **Glossary:** company, product, and industry terms the user should recognize and use correctly.

## Output (files in the round folder)

```
Briefing.md          — 1 page: company snapshot, interviewers, format, top 3 things to land, watch-outs
Stories.md           — 4–6 STAR stories, each tagged to likely questions
Questions-to-ask.md  — questions grouped by interviewer/topic
Glossary.md          — company/product/industry terms
Positioning.md       — why me / why this role / why now (script)
```
