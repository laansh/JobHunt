# Scoring Rubric

## Hard gates (override the score)
- Must-haves: owns a product line or P&L; eng team ≥ 6; remote or Denver-hybrid
- Dealbreakers: 5-day on-site; title is really program/project mgmt; pre-revenue < 12 mo runway

## Weighted dimensions
| Dimension | Weight | 5 = | 3 = | 1 = |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Compensation | 25 | ≥ $200k base | $185–199k base | < $185k base |
| Scope / level | 25 | Owns multiple squads + strategy | Owns one squad | IC PM, no reports |
| Domain fit | 20 | B2B SaaS / fintech-adjacent | Adjacent vertical | Off-target (ad-tech, etc.) |
| Growth path | 15 | Clear director track < 2 yrs | Possible, undefined | Flat / capped |
| Company health | 10 | Series C–D, growing revenue | Series B, stable | Unclear runway |
| Remote/location | 5 | Fully remote | Denver hybrid | Relocation required |
(weights sum to 100)

## Scoring
- Weighted total = Σ (dimension score 1–5 × weight) / 100
- Tiers:
  - **Pursue (deep eval):** ≥ 3.6
  - **Maybe / revisit:** 2.8 – 3.5
  - **Pass:** < 2.8
